Rakata Memorial

In Honor of the Infinite Empire

We remember the mighty Rakata, whose infinite empire spanned the galaxy. Their mastery of the Force and advanced technology brought them to the heights of power.

Predor Tul'kar

Greatest military commander of the Infinite Empire's Golden Age. Led countless successful campaigns across the galaxy.

Force Hound Xesh

Legendary warrior who served the Rakata with unwavering loyalty. Master of both combat and Force techniques.

Predor Skal'nas

Architect of the Empire's expansion into the Unknown Regions. Known for his tactical brilliance and ruthlessness.

May their legacy of power and conquest never be forgotten. Their sacrifices paved the way for the Sith Empire.
