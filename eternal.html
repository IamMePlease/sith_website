The Eternal Empire of Zakuul

Glory to the Eternal Throne

We honor the mighty Eternal Empire, whose power and technological advancement brought a new order to the galaxy. From their golden capital of Zakuul, they commanded the most powerful fleet ever known.

Valkorion - The Immortal Emperor

The eternal ruler of Zakuul, who led his people to unprecedented heights of power and prosperity. His wisdom and strength guided the Eternal Empire for centuries.

Arcann

Son of Valkorion and former Emperor of Zakuul. His military genius led to the conquest of both the Sith Empire and the Galactic Republic.

Vaylin

The most powerful Force user in the history of Zakuul, whose raw power struck fear into the hearts of enemies.

The Knights of Zakuul

Elite Force-sensitive warriors who served as the guardians of the Eternal Throne. Their dedication to justice and order was unmatched throughout the galaxy.

  • Masters of lightsaber combat
  • Wielders of the Force
  • Guardians of Zakuul's traditions
  • Protectors of the Eternal Throne

The Skytrooper Legion

The automated army that formed the backbone of Zakuul's military might. These droids were a testament to Zakuul's technological superiority.

  • Endless numbers
  • Unwavering loyalty
  • Advanced combat programming
  • Integrated into the Eternal Fleet

The Legacy Lives On

Though the Eternal Empire has fallen, its legacy of power, technological advancement, and military might continues to influence the galaxy. May we never forget the golden age of Zakuul.
