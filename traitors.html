Traitors to the Sith Order

Darth Vader (Anakin Skywalker)

Ultimate betrayal: Killed Emperor Palpatine to save his son, Luke Skywalker

"Just for once, let me look on you with my own eyes."
  • Abandoned the dark side at the moment of triumph
  • Destroyed decades of Sith planning
  • Brought balance to the Force, ending Sith dominance

Fate: Died after returning to the light side

Darth Traya (Kreia)

Betrayal: Sought to destroy the Force itself

"To be united by hatred is a fragile alliance at best."
  • Attempted to end both Sith and Jedi
  • Manipulated events to try to destroy the Force
  • Trained both Jedi and Sith while serving neither

Fate: Killed by the Exile at Malachor V

Darth Nihilus

Betrayal: Abandoned Sith ideology for pure consumption

"He is a wound in the Force, more presence than flesh."
  • Consumed entire worlds without purpose
  • Betrayed the Sith Triumvirate
  • Became a force of nature rather than a true Sith

Fate: Destroyed by the Exile and Visas Marr

Darth Gravid

Betrayal: Attempted to combine Jedi and Sith teachings

  • Destroyed countless Sith artifacts and teachings
  • Compromised the Rule of Two
  • Nearly ended the Sith lineage

Fate: Killed by his apprentice, Darth Gean

Darth Millennial

Betrayal: Rejected the Rule of Two

  • Founded the Prophets of the Dark Side
  • Created his own dark side philosophy
  • Abandoned his master's teachings

Fate: Unknown

Darth Vectivus

Betrayal: Refused to embrace true darkness

  • Maintained moral principles despite being Sith
  • Used dark side powers with restraint
  • Failed to advance Sith goals

Fate: Died peacefully of old age

Githany

Betrayal: Multiple betrayals of both Jedi and Sith

  • Betrayed the Jedi Order
  • Betrayed fellow Sith during the New Sith Wars
  • Attempted to manipulate both sides

Fate: Died in the Thought Bomb on Ruusan

Darth Revan

Complex betrayal: Returned to the light side and later opposed both Jedi and Sith

  • Abandoned the Sith Empire he created
  • Fought against the Emperor
  • Sought balance rather than dark side dominance
