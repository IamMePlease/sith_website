1. Introduction

The Sith Purebloods represent one of the most significant species in galactic history, whose influence on the Force and galactic politics continues to resonate through the millennia. Originally native to the planets Korriban and Ziost, these red-skinned humanoids developed a sophisticated culture centered around the dark side of the Force, eventually giving their name to the entire dark side tradition that would come to be known as the Sith Order.

"We were mighty once, and our legacy endures in the blood of those who still bear our mark." - Ancient Sith text

2. Origins and Early History

2.1 Ancient Origins

The Sith species evolved on Korriban, a harsh world saturated with dark side energy. Their early civilization developed in isolation, shaped by their planet's extreme conditions and the omnipresent influence of the dark side. Archaeological evidence suggests that primitive Sith societies existed as far back as 100,000 BBY.

2.2 Pre-Republic Era

Before contact with the wider galaxy, the Sith developed a sophisticated feudal society, with powerful Force-sensitive bloodlines ruling over the general population. Their mastery of alchemy and sorcery allowed them to create remarkable technological and architectural achievements, many of which still stand on Korriban to this day.

2.3 First Contact

The arrival of the Dark Jedi exiles following the Second Great Schism marked a turning point in Sith history. These powerful Force users conquered and interbred with the native Sith, leading to significant genetic and cultural changes in the species.

3. Biology and Physiology

3.1 Physical Characteristics

Sith Purebloods are characterized by their red skin, facial tendrils, yellow eyes, and distinctive bone structure. Their biology is naturally attuned to the dark side of the Force, with many displaying inherent Force sensitivity.

  • Height: Generally taller than baseline humans
  • Skin: Various shades of red
  • Eyes: Naturally yellow or orange
  • Distinctive facial features including bone spurs and tendrils
  • Enhanced strength and durability

3.2 Genetic Adaptations

Millennia of evolution on Korriban led to several unique adaptations: - Natural resistance to dark side corruption - Enhanced healing capabilities - Superior night vision - Heightened Force sensitivity

4. Society and Culture

4.1 Social Structure

Sith society was strictly hierarchical, based on Force sensitivity and bloodline purity. The species developed complex customs around power, inheritance, and Force usage.
