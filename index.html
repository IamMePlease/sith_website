The Path of the Sith

The Sith Code

Peace is a lie, there is only passion.
Through passion, I gain strength.
Through strength, I gain power.
Through power, I gain victory.
Through victory, my chains are broken.
The Force shall free me.

Guidelines to Becoming a Sith

  • Embrace your emotions and let them fuel your power
  • Study the dark side of the Force
  • Master lightsaber combat
  • Learn to channel your hatred and anger
  • Seek absolute power
  • Challenge yourself and overcome limitations
  • Eliminate weakness
  • Trust no one but yourself
